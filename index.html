Explore the different sizes and murals as much as you like. When you go to Calculate, you'll start with one of the small murals and work your way through the larger sizes.

Start by using a paintbrush to color in the small painting. Then measure its dimensions, calculate its area, and observe how many buckets of paint you used.

When you are ready, click "Check Answers" to reveal if all your answers are correct. If your estimations are close to the exact answer, they will be marked correct with a green check and they will be changed to an exact number.

If they are all correct, a "Next" button will appear and you'll move on to a medium-sized version of painting.
